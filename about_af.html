About us

WORDS FROM THE FOUNDER

Myself Kavita founder of Aram Seivom Trust, I was working for physically challenged students for nearly 1 year , During my work I felt their sufferings and pains what they are going through their day to day life . Due to my personal commitment I have to leave my NGO job I decided to spend entire life for Physically challenged people by doing service for them , I registered my trust in the name Aram Seivom Trust. As a team we contribute money & do services for physically challenged , MR kids , Underprivileged people in our society ,We are also adopting government schools for developing infrastructure , Also medical related services like Conducting Eyes camps, Blood camps ,Free surgery's for patients on month on month basis.

FUTURE VISION : Adopting government schools for better advancement of infrastructure. Helping physically challenged people.
